KINETIC TYPOGRAPHY

Kinetic Typography (Audio Used is from "Tron: Legacy."

PERSONAL WORK


Role

Motion Graphics Designer

Project

Demonstrate knowledge of Adobe After Effects with a comprehensible visual flow of text, meaning, and audio/video synchronization.

Solution

Create an advanced Kinetic Typography video that will synchronize with an audio excerpt selected from a movie. The visuals in this project used no outside software or plugins. Complete project is created inside of Adobe After Effects.

Technologies / Skills

  • Adobe After Effects
  • Audio/Video Production
  • Storyboarding
  • Design Principles

What I Did

  • Determined best settings for video/output.
  • Viewed Inspiration and brainstormed ideas.
  • Selected audio excerpt that conforms to estimated video time.
  • Determined final concept to use in project.
  • Storyboard video with settings/data for all synchronization points.
  • Determined camera angles for each scene.
  • Decided on special effects to apply to text in each scene.
  • Animated typography
  • Synchronized video with audio. (scrubbed audio to fine-tune synchronization)
  • Rendered composition.
